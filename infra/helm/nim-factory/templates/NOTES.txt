NIM Factory is installed.

Port-forward the service:
  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "nim-factory.fullname" . }} 8001:{{ .Values.service.port }}

Call the factory:
  curl -sS -X POST http://localhost:8001/factory/ingest \
    -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
    -d '{"model_name":"nvidia/nemotron-3-nano-omni-30b-a3b-reasoning","target_gpu":"NVIDIA GB200","target_environment":"kubernetes"}' | jq .
